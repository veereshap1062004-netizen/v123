[Your Name]
[Your Address]
[City, State, Pincode]
[Date]

[Recipient's Name]
[Recipient's Designation]
[Company/Organization Name]
[Company/Organization Address]
[City, State, Pincode]

Subject: Application for Leave due to Health Issues

Dear [Recipient's Name],

I am writing to inform you that I will be taking a leave of absence from work for 5 days, from [start date] to [end date], due to some health issues that I am currently facing. My doctor has advised me to take complete rest and undergo treatment for the next few days, and I will be unavailable to come to work during this period.

I apologize for any inconvenience this may cause and will ensure that all my tasks are completed as soon as possible upon my return to work. I will also be available by email if anything urgent comes up while I am away.

I have attached a medical certificate from my doctor to this letter, which confirms my illness and the need for me to take leave. If there is any additional information or documentation required, please let me know.

Thank you for your understanding and support during this time. If you have any questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to reach out to me.

Sincerely,

[Your Signature]
[Your Name]

Note: Please attach a medical certificate from your doctor to the letter, if possible. Also, make sure to customize the letter with your own details and the specific requirements of your company or organization.
