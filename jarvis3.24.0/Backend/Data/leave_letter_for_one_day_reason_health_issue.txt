Here is a sample leave letter for one day due to a health issue:

[Your Name]
[Your Address]
[City, State, PIN]
[Date]

[Recipient's Name]
[Recipient's Designation]
[Company/Organization Name]
[Company Address]
[City, State, PIN]

Subject: Leave Application for One Day due to Health Issue

Dear [Recipient's Name],

I am writing to inform you that I will be unable to attend work today, [Date], due to a sudden health issue that requires my immediate attention. I am currently under medical care and need to take a day's rest to recover.

I apologize for any inconvenience this may cause and will make sure to catch up on any missed work as soon as possible. I will be available by email if anything urgent comes up during my absence.

I would like to request a leave of absence for one day, [Date], and will resume my duties from tomorrow, [Next Day's Date]. I will provide a medical certificate, if required, to support my leave application.

Thank you for your understanding and support.

Sincerely,

[Your Name]

Your satisfaction is my top priority; feel free to reach out if there's anything else I can help you with.
